006 — Favorite AI
🤖

My AI of
choice? Claude.

I've tried them all. Claude by Anthropic is the one I keep coming back to. It thinks carefully, writes with nuance, and doesn't hallucinate its way through complex questions. It's the AI I trust when the output actually matters.

I use Claude daily — for brainstorming product strategy, debugging Python scripts, drafting essays, building this very website, and wiring up my personal AI agent. It's the closest thing I've found to a genuinely thoughtful collaborator.

Anthropic's focus on AI safety and building AI that is helpful, harmless, and honest aligns with how I think technology should be built. Good values in the model, good values in the company.
